My Dearest Husband

My Love,

Five years ago, I made the best decision of my life — I said "I do" to you. And every single day since, I've fallen in love with you all over again. You are not just my husband; you are my best friend, my safe harbour, and my greatest adventure.

Thank you for your unwavering love, for holding our family together with such quiet strength and warmth. Thank you for the late nights, the sacrifices you never complained about, and the endless way you show up — for me, for our children, for this home we've built together.

Watching you be a father has made me love you even more than I thought possible. Our children are so lucky to have you, and so am I.

亲爱的，感谢你五年来对我无微不至的爱与包容。
感谢你为这个家庭所付出的一切——你的辛劳、你的坚持、你的温柔。
感谢你成为孩子们最好的爸爸，也是我最坚实的依靠。
有你在身边，每一天都充满意义。
结婚五周年快乐，我爱你，今生今世。 ❤️

Forever yours 🌹
